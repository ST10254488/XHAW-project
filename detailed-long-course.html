Details on 6 Month Courses

First Aid Training Course

Fees: R1500

Purpose :

This course is designed to equip participants with essential first aid skills, including life-saving techniques and emergency response. It aims to build awareness of how to manage common injuries and accidents while offering basic life support.

What you will learn :

  • Wounds and Bleeding: How to control bleeding, treat wounds, and prevent infection.
  • Burns and Fractures: Effective treatment of burns and fractures to reduce complications before professional help arrives.
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR): Master the technique of performing CPR on individuals who are unconscious or not breathing.
  • Emergency Scene Management: Steps to safely and efficiently manage an emergency scene, prioritizing the safety of the injured and those around them.

By the end of this course, participants will have the confidence to act quickly and effectively during medical emergencies, potentially saving lives.

Sewing Course

Fees: R1500

Purpose :

This course aims to empower participants with the skills to perform clothing alterations and create custom-tailored garments. Whether you're looking to mend and adjust existing clothes or design new ones from scratch, this course will provide the foundational knowledge you need.

What you will learn :

  • Types of Stitches: Learn the most commonly used stitches, from basic hand stitches to more complex machine stitches.
  • Threading a Sewing Machine: Master the process of correctly threading a sewing machine for various fabrics and projects.
  • Alterations: Understand how to alter garments, such as adjusting the fit, repairing worn-out pieces, or resizing clothing.
  • Sewing Buttons, Zips, Hems, and Seams: Gain practical skills in attaching buttons, zippers, and creating clean hems and seams for a professional finish.

By the end of this course, participants will have the confidence to act quickly and effectively during medical emergencies, potentially saving lives.

Landscaping Course

Fees: R1500

Purpose :

This course is designed to provide participants with the essential knowledge and skills to offer professional landscaping services. It focuses on creating and maintaining visually appealing and well-balanced gardens, whether for new installations or existing spaces.

What you will learn :

  • Indigenous / Exotic Plants and Trees: Learn to identify and care for both local and exotic plant species, understanding their growth patterns and environmental needs.
  • Balancing Plants and Trees: Understand the principles of plant placement and tree positioning to achieve a harmonious and well-balanced garden environment.
  • Garden Layout: Learn how to design efficient, practical, and aesthetically pleasing garden layouts that optimize space, functionality, and natural beauty.
  • Fixed Structures: Gain insight into incorporating fixed structures like fountains, statues, benches, tables, and built-in braais to enhance the functionality and beauty of a garden.li>

By the end of this course, participants will have the confidence to design, plan, and execute landscaping projects that enhance the beauty and value of both residential and commercial gardens.

Life Skills Course

Fees: R1500

Purpose :

This course is aimed at equipping participants with essential life skills that help them manage daily challenges more effectively. It covers practical knowledge that improves personal independence and empowerment in navigating key aspects of life.

What you will learn :

  • Opening a Bank Account: Understand the steps and documentation needed to open and manage a bank account, helping participants manage their finances more securely.
  • Basic Reading and Writing Literacy: Gain foundational literacy skills, allowing participants to read and write for practical, everyday use such as filling out forms, reading instructions, and communicating effectively.
  • Basic Numeric Literacy: Improve the ability to understand and use numbers for everyday tasks such as budgeting, managing bills, and handling transactions.
  • Basic Labor Law (Know Your Rights): Learn about fundamental labor rights, including employment contracts, working conditions, and fair treatment, empowering participants to protect themselves in the workplace.

By the end of this course, participants will have the confidence to design, plan, and execute landscaping projects that enhance the beauty and value of both residential and commercial gardens.
