Six-Week Courses

In addition to our six-month programs, Empowering the Nation offers intensive six-week courses designed to provide rapid skill enhancement in high-demand areas. These shorter courses are perfect for individuals looking to expand their skillset quickly, offering valuable knowledge that can be immediately applied in the workplace or at home.

Our six-week courses are designed to be fast-paced, hands-on, and practical, allowing participants to quickly gain the skills they need to improve their job prospects or take on new responsibilities. With a focus on delivering high-quality training in a short time frame, these courses provide immediate value to participants and their employers, helping to create more skilled and efficient workers.

6 Week courses offered :

  • Cooking
  • Garden Maintenance
