Details on 6 Week Courses

Child Minding Course

Fees: R750

Purpose :

This course is designed to provide participants with fundamental skills in child and baby care, focusing on understanding the developmental needs of children from birth to toddler age. It offers practical knowledge for those caring for infants and young children, ensuring their safety, health, and well-being.

What you will learn :

  • Birth to Six-Month-Old Baby Needs: Learn how to care for newborns and infants, including feeding, sleeping routines, and understanding the basics of baby health and hygiene.
  • Seven-Month to One-Year-Old Needs: Discover how to support babies as they become more mobile, focusing on nutrition, motor skills development, and baby-proofing the environment.
  • Toddler Needs: Understand the developmental milestones of toddlers, including managing behavior, encouraging language development, and providing balanced nutrition for growing children.
  • Educational Toys: Gain insight into selecting appropriate educational toys that stimulate a childâ€™s cognitive, emotional, and physical development at different stages.

By the end of this course, participants will have the knowledge and confidence to provide attentive, nurturing care to children, ensuring their development in a safe and supportive environment.

Cooking Course

Fees: R750

Purpose :

This course aims to provide participants with the essential skills to prepare and cook nutritious, balanced meals for families. It focuses on understanding the basics of nutrition, meal planning, and practical cooking techniques to ensure healthy eating habits.

What you will learn :

  • Nutritional Requirements for a Healthy Body: Learn about the key nutrients the body needs, including vitamins, minerals, and other essential dietary components to promote overall health.
  • Types of Protein, Carbohydrates, and Vegetables: Understand the different types of proteins, carbohydrates, and vegetables, and how to incorporate them into a balanced diet.
  • Planning Meals: Gain skills in planning wholesome meals that meet the nutritional needs of all family members, taking into consideration portion control and variety.
  • Preparation and Cooking of Meals: Learn practical cooking techniques for preparing a range of dishes, from simple everyday meals to more complex recipes, with a focus on maximizing nutrition while keeping flavors delicious.

By the end of this course, participants will have the confidence to create nutritious, well-balanced meals that support a healthy lifestyle for families.

Garden Maintenance Course

Fees: R750

Purpose :

This course provides participants with fundamental skills in maintaining a healthy, thriving domestic garden. It focuses on proper watering, pruning, and planting techniques that help ensure the long-term beauty and sustainability of garden spaces.

What you will learn :

  • Water Restrictions and the Watering Requirements of Indigenous and Exotic Plants: Understand local water restrictions and how to care for plants with varying water needs, focusing on both indigenous and exotic species.
  • Pruning and Propagation of Plants: Learn the techniques for proper pruning to encourage healthy growth, as well as methods for propagating plants to expand your garden.
  • Planting Techniques for Different Plant Types: Gain practical knowledge of how to plant a variety of plant species, from flowers to shrubs and trees, ensuring they are positioned and planted correctly for optimal growth.

By the end of this course, participants will have the skills to manage garden maintenance tasks effectively, contributing to the long-term health and beauty of domestic gardens.
